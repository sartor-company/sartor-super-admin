How Sticker Orders Work
📄
1. Client Orders
Client places sticker order via their admin. Sartor auto-applies 10% overage.
🔑
2. PIN Generation
Samuel (AI/ML) generates DORMANT 10-digit alphanumeric PINs. Activated at batch activation.
📷
3. QR Code Gen
QR codes generated and linked to batch. Each QR encodes the verify URL + batch token.
⇩
4. Download Package
Ops downloads: PIN manifest CSV and QR PNG/SVG files. Delivered to print vendor.
🚚
5. Print & Dispatch
Printed stickers dispatched by courier (GIG/DHL) to client. Tracking number logged here.
